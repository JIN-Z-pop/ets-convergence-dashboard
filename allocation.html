Allocation Evolution

How each country allocates emission allowances — from Grandfathering to Auctioning

Allocation Method Migration Path

EU: Grandfathering (Phase 1-2) → BM + Auction (Phase 3) → CBAM-linked full auctioning (Phase 4). The most mature system, approaching 100% paid allocation for CBAM sectors by 2034.

Korea: Started with 100% GF (Phase 1), introduced BM for select sectors (Phase 2), now at 90% free / 10% auction (Phase 3). Gradual but steady transition.

China: Intensity-based BM from Day 1. 100% free allocation. No auction mechanism yet. Expanding from power-only to 4 sectors (steel, cement, aluminum) in 2025.

Japan: GX League voluntary targets (2023). GX-ETS Phase 2 (2026) may introduce mandatory caps. Auction planned from Phase 3 (2033).
