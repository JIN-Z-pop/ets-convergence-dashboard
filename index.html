About This Dashboard

This dashboard visualizes the convergence and divergence of Emission Trading Systems (ETS) across four major economies — EU, Korea, China, and Japan — as they approach 2030. Data is sourced from the gods-eye-db (48 tables, ~26,300 rows) and Knowledge Brain RAG (1,600+ items).

Navigate using the top bar to explore system comparisons, allocation evolution, CBAM impacts, convergence mapping, and a 4-language ETS terminology reference.
